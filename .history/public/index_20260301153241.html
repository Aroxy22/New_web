💌 A Letter for You, Mumma

Dear Mumma,

Today is your birthday, and I just wanted to pause and tell you, from my heart, how much you mean to me. No words can fully capture it, but this is my small attempt to thank you for all the love you’ve given over the years.

What I admire most about you is your endless, unconditional love. You always put our family first—your warmth, your hugs, your late nights, and all the small things you quietly do for us every single day. You face every situation with strength and grace, and you’ve shown me that real strength is soft, patient, and full of love.

Your smile has a magic of its own. It can turn a bad day into a better one in a second. The way you listen, care, and understand without me having to say much makes me feel safe and loved. Because of you, our house is not just a place—it’s a home filled with warmth, laughter, and comfort.

You have sacrificed so much for us, often without saying a word. From the biggest decisions to the smallest details, you always think about what will make us happier and more comfortable. Through you, I have learned the true meaning of kindness, patience, and what it means to love someone with your whole heart.

Thank you for being my safe place, my biggest supporter, and my constant blessing. Everything good in me has a part of you in it. I am so proud to be your child and so grateful for every moment, every lesson, and every bit of love you’ve given me.

Happy Birthday, Mumma. May this year bring you good health, peace, and all the happiness you deserve. You are loved more than words can ever say. 💕

With all my love,
Aryan ❤️
